▸ Learn: How It Works
📡 VES — Vertical Electrical Sounding Measures how electricity passes through soil layers. Higher resistance often means drier, more compact ground. Your 11 data points are survey locations around FUTO used to model the subsurface terrain.
▸ Generative AI Role
🤖 What Gen AI Does Here The terrain between your 11 survey points is generated by IDW interpolation — a spatial AI algorithm. Environmental simulations (flood, erosion) use procedural noise to create realistic-looking change patterns from minimal data.
